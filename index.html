Publications & Research Experience

3DGS

Towards Robust Sparse-View 3D Gaussian Splatting via Hierarchical Depth and Multi-View Consistency

National Undergraduate Innovation Project · ACM ICMR 2026 · CCF-B

Role: Project Lead & Core Contributor

Proposed SegGeoGS, a hierarchical depth regularization framework for sparse-view 3D Gaussian Splatting. The method integrates foreground segmentation masks, depth-prior supervision, random Gaussian dropout, and multi-view consistency constraints to improve geometric reconstruction and rendering fidelity.

Real2Sim: Interactive Urban Scene Instantiation

Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences · IIP-VIPL Lab · Nov 2025 – Jun 2026

Supervisors: Prof. Shiguang Shan & Zhenliang He

Worked on UrbanVerse, a retrieval-generation continual-learning framework driven by semantic-similarity-based hybrid decisions. Qingyou Quantum Team · Apr 2024 – Present · Applied Physics Letters / Chinese Physics B

Role: 1st / 2nd Author
